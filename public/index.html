(Rules, in case we forgot)

Yes, it's that game. The one humans have been arguing about since before computers were asked to settle it.
You pick one of three options: Rock - Paper - Scissors Then the rules do what they've always done, regardless of your confidence level:
Rock crushes Scissors
Scissors cuts Paper
Paper covers Rock
If both players pick the same thing, nothing happens except disappointment and a rematch.
Notes from Gramps - There is no secret fourth option - No, you can't “think harder” to change the outcome - Yes, you will still lose to randomness occasionally, and no, the universe will not compensate you for it Good luck.
